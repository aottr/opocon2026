## Parking

![Freizeithaus Petershof Travel guide parking](/img/parking.png)
{.align-left}

Guests arriving by car can park along the curve on Oststraße, not far from Petershof. 

We kindly ask all participants to only drive to directly up the venue to load or unload the car.

## By Train

![Freizeithaus Petershof Travel guide train](/img/train.png)
{.align-right}

Train connections to **Erbach (Odw.) train station** are very regular and therefore easily accessible. Once you have made it this far, we will be happy to pick you up with a shuttle. Please let us know half an hour in advance.
