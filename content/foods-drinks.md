{{< info-banner >}}

OpoCon will provide you with a variety of food and drinks, all included in the ticket price!

{{< /info-banner >}}

**Long Breakfast:** A good **breakfast** will be provided every day from **Friday to Monday**, from around **9:00** to **noon**.

**Afternoon snack:** We will have a little afternoon snack such as ice cream, cake, or waffles each day.

**Dinner:** We will have a **fresh, warm dinner** for everyone to enjoy at around **17:00** every day. What exactly? Have a look below.

**Cheese & Wine Tasting:** What would Opo be without cheese? **Nothing!** For the famous cheese and wine tasting, you can bring your own delicacies and offer them to all the participants.

**Also:** Want to stuff yourself with food **after midnight?** There will be **leftovers** for you to grab and enjoy, just keep everything clean. We will also provide smaller snacks like chips, popcorn, etc.

We offer **vegetarian and vegan** options so you don’t have to worry about ever going hungry.

Of course our guests have to stay hydrated. Here is a list of drinks we offer at OpoCon (again: **all included** in the general ticket price!)

- Orange- and apple juice, Ice Tea
- Cola, Cola Zero, Fanta, Sprite
