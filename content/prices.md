### About Sponsor Upgrade
{.text-center}

With your booked sponsor upgrade, you will receive **a comprehensive and unique package** with extras that will only be available **once per person!** Of the 35€ for the upgrade, OpoCon will keep ~10€, which will be reinvested in the convention at the end. 

**So thank you very much for your support!**
