## Freizeithaus Petershof
<p class="headline-subtext">Talweg 5 · 65711 Erbach · Germany</p>

![Freizeithaus Petershof](/img/petershof.jpg)
{.align-right}

Petershof offers OpoCon the perfect location for up to 60 participants to celebrate, relax, and simply having fun. The approximately 1,000 m² facility offers more than enough space on three floors for everyone to retreat to. Thanks to the dining room, kitchen, and a large event hall, we can cater for ourselves at a reasonable price.

The courtyard offers us great event opportunities, and the 5,000 m² of nearby meadowland by the forest can also be used for more athletic activities. A small campfire should also be possible here.

## The Rooms

![Freizeithaus Petershof Rooms](/img/rooms.jpg)
{.align-left}

The 14 rooms, each with 2 to 8 beds, offer enough space and comfort for small and large groups. The furnishings are simple but functional, and there are sufficient clean sanitary facilities.

Rooms will initially be allocated by the OpoCon staff. Subsequent allocation and reassignment of rooms will be made in consultation with OpoCon staff after its announcement of the occupation plan.
